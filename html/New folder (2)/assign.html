Baking is a method of cooking food that uses prolonged dry heat, normally in an oven, but also in hot ashes, or on hot stones. The most common baked item is bread but many other types of foods are baked.Heat is gradually transferred "from the surface of cakes, cookies, and breads to their center. About Me

So you want to find out a little bit more about me, huh? Who is this woman and should I trust her recipes? Can she cook? Why is she so tiny?! Okay then! Let’s get started. I’m Razeena, living here in good old sunshine state. I’m a food lover who likes costume dramas, miniature things, movie series and sleeping to the sound of rain.
I started this blog in April, 2015 as a creative outlet for me to express my love for all things food, eating and cooking. I’m the kind of person who will simmer with excitement over something I picked from an online menu before I go and actually eat it at a restaurant. I’ll taste it in my head, drool alone while doing some imaginary tasting and then unleash my inner ravenous monster when I actually get there. It’s fun.
